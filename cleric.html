Cleric Features

  • Hit Dice: 1d8 per cleric level
  • Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
  • Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 4) + your Constitution modifier per cleric level after 1st

Proficiencies

  • Armor: Light armor, medium armor, shields
  • Weapons: All simple weapons
  • Tools: None
  • Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma
  • Skills: You'll be able to choose two (2) from History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion

Spellcasting Ability

As a conduit for divine power, you can cast cleric spells. At 1st level, you know three cantrips of your choice from the cleric spell list. You have two, 1st level spell slots available at cleric level one. Unlike Wizards, clerics do not need a spellbook to store their spells, and are assumed to know every cleric spell their spell slots can afford them.

Ritual Casting

You can cast a cleric spell as a ritual if that spell has the ritual tag and you have the spell prepared.

Spellcasting Focus

You can use a holy symbol as a spellcasting focus for your cleric spells.
